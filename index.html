About Kalinga Fellowship

The Kalinga Fellowship is a programme that brings together progressive leaders from business, government and civil society to facilitate breakthroughs in thinking and action in the combatting of human trafficking. Through collaboration of different stakeholders, the Fellowship aims to build an ecosystem to combat the malaise of human trafficking in India.
